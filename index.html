--- ABOUT ---

Mission statement: 'The Bal Bharati Vidyamandir aims to strive for the all round development of the child and to become alert, enlightened and responsible citizens.'


'Bal Bharati vidyamandir' the very name reflects the ideology of the founders that the school will be a temple of learning and all efforts will be aimed at making the school a miniature model of Bharat and thus the institution aims to bring about the all round development of children's personality and to develop the institution into an ideal 'community school based on mutual co-operation between the school and the local community, This institution was established on 18th June 1962, the Revolution Day, immediately after the Goa liberation on 19th December 1961 and was managed by Bal Bharati Vidyamandir Trust founded by shri Gurunath V.Shetye of Raibandar...
